Bot Features

What it can do

Provide general vehicle info

This is available for any UK Bus that is registered to the API the bot uses. Information varies from the vehicle registration, fleet number, operator,  emission type to withdrawal status

Track London Buses LIVE

Shows the next stop they are due at, the Route and Destination, as well as how long it will take to get to the next stop.

Provide Vehicle Imagery

Based on your vehicle information request, the bot will search for photos of the bus, so that you can ensure your search is accurate

Track vehicles on a route

The bot has the ability to track vehicles on a route, their stop and destination. It also provides the ETA and operator

Uses slash commands

You're at ease with slash commands instead of prefixes
